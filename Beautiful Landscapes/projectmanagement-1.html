PROJECT MANAGEMENT

WHY I CHOSE A CAREER IN PROJECT MANAGEMENT


This field involves organizing and overseeing projects to achieve specific goals within a defined timeframe and budget. Many people find it fulfilling because it allows them to apply their organizational and leadership skills to bring projects to successful completion. It can also be a dynamic and varied field with opportunities for growth and advancement. There are so many career paths that you can take within this field. For example, E-commerce Project Manager, Health IT Project Manager, Cloud Project Manager Agile Coach or Scrum Master and a Program Manager.
